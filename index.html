About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio. I co-lead the AI Digital Infrastructure Center of Excellence at Boston Consulting Group, where I advise leading organizations in the semiconductor, AI, and data center industries.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with diverse clients, helping them navigate the complex landscape of artificial intelligence infrastructure and semiconductor strategies. I believe in a data-driven approach to consulting that combines technical expertise with business acumen.

I hold degrees from UC Berkeley and the University of Virginia, which have equipped me with a strong foundation in both technical and business domains. My work spans from strategic planning and market analysis to implementation of cutting-edge AI infrastructure solutions.

When I'm not working with clients, you can find me traveling and exploring new destinations around the world, always seeking to understand different markets and technological ecosystems globally.
